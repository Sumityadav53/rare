Bugatti Type 57SC Atlantic (1936-1938)

The rare and iconic grand tourer produced by Bugatti from 1936 to 1938.

Specifications

  • Engine: 3.3-liter inline-eight with supercharger
  • Horsepower: 210 hp
  • Top Speed: Over 200 km/h (124 mph)
  • Transmission: 4-speed manual

History

Three units were sold to private customers: Rothschild Atlantic (chassis 57374), Holzschuh Atlantic (chassis 57473), and Pope Atlantic (chassis 57591) The fourth unit, chassis 57453, was retained by Jean Bugatti, the son of Ettore Bugatti, and its current whereabouts remain unknown
