Rolls-Royce 15 hp Model

a rare and historic vehicle produced by Royce Ltd., the company founded by Henry Royce.

Specifications

  • Engine: 3.0-liter, 3-cylinder
  • Horsepower: 15 hp
  • Top Speed: 39 mph (63 km/h)
  • Transmission: 3-speed

History

The Rolls-Royce 15 hp was one of four cars produced as a result of an agreement between Charles Rolls and Henry Royce in December 1904. The partnership led to the formation of Rolls-Royce, although the 15 hp was credited solely to Royce’s company, “Royce Limited.” The vehicle’s design and engineering reflect Royce’s innovative approach, which emphasized performance, durability, and style.

Features

The Rolls-Royce 15 hp is notable for its stunning appearance and impressive speed, reportedly making it one of the fastest cars of its era. Its rarity and historical significance have made it a prized possession for collectors and enthusiasts.
