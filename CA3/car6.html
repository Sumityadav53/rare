McLaren F1 LM (1995)

Originally, only five LM models were made to honor McLaren’s victory at Le Mans.

Specifications

  • Engine: BMW S70/2 V12, 6.1L
  • Horsepower: 680 hp
  • Top Speed: 220 mph (354 km/h)
  • Transmission: 6-speed manual

History

The McLaren F1 LM is a highly sought-after collector’s item, with its rarity and racing heritage making it a prized possession for enthusiasts.

Features

  • Stripped of unnecessary elements, such as carpeting, stereo, and air conditioning
  • Only six units produced, with five sold and one retained by McLaren for their museum
  • Estimated to be two to three times more expensive than the standard McLaren F1, which sold for over $815,000 in 1995
  • Rear spoiler with larger endplates featuring the GTR logo and “24 Heures du Mans Winners 1995” script
